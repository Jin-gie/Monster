Actions

You can apply different actions to the monster. These actions will affect the life and/or the money of the monster. You can find the description of these actions below:
  • Run
    • Withdraw 1 hp
  • Fight
    • Withdraw 3 hp
  • Work
    • Withdraw 1 hp
    • Add 2 coins
  • Eat
    • Withdraw 3 coins
    • Add 2 hp

Money color code

From 60 of money, the circle around the Tomat' guy will change color according to levels of money:
  • █ between 60 and 99
  • █ between 100 and 149
  • █ between 150 and 199
  • █ higher than 200
